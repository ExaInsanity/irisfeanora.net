Minecraft 1.15.1

Minecraft 1.15.1 is a mostly abandoned version - which is quite understandable, considering it is not the final release of the 1.15 cycle and only few people actually use the 1.15 release cycle to begin with.

However, 1.15.1 is also a great version especially for technical gameplay, therefore some of us have decided to just... maintain it.

Contributors

Projects

Vanadium

Vanadium is a server-side mod applying some tweaks to various parts of the game. It adds no new features.

Palladium

Palladium is a client-side mod applying several tweaks to the interfaces. It adds no new features.


Further planned mods include: VillagerTools, a server-and-client mod to drastically enhance villagers.


The suite also includes several more extreme mods, for example a mod by Zefyro backporting blackstone to 1.15.1.
